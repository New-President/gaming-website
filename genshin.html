About Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is the top 10 most played action/role-playing mobile games developed by a company based in China named as MiHoYo. Genshin Impact is also available on PC as well as PS4/PS5!

There are many exciting characters for you to obtain and play with in a team of four of your own composition. This action packed game surely does not miss a beat!

With 4 large regions for you to explore, players can expect a riveting storyline packed with lores and many hours of gameplay accompanied by fantastic soundtracks that immerses you in the environment! Check out their amazing band!
